How Much Will the Lower USDA Fees Save You?

USDA announced they are reducing both the upfront Guarantee fee and the Annual mortgage insurance fee (paid monthly) for purchase and refinance loans. Lower costs for the $0 down USDA loan will help make USDA financing for rural homebuyers even more affordable.

The reduced USDA insurance fee begin October 1st, 2016 and extend out through to September 31, 2017.
